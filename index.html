Title Blazing Saddles
Director + Producer Mel Brooks + Michael Hertzberg
Leading Actor(s) Cleavon Little and Gene Wilder
Leading Actress Madeline Kahn
Rating R. Read a review here.
Description Blazing Saddles is a classic spoofy comedy with bawdy language, innuendo, and a send up of racism. Crafty railroad worker Bart becomes the first black sheriff of Rock Ridge, a frontier town about to be destroyed in order to make way for a new railroad. Bart and his perpetually drunk gunfighter friend are the only defense against a wave of thugs sent to rid the town of its population.
